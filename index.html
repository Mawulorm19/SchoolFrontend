Academics

Excellence in Education at Every Level



Nursery

Engaging activities that develop creativity, social skills, and a love for learning in young minds.

Learn More

Our Nursery program offers a nurturing environment where children engage in hands-on activities, creative play, and early learning experiences. We focus on developing social, emotional, and cognitive skills to prepare young learners for future academic success.

Kindergarten

Preparing students for High School and beyond through rigorous academics and leadership opportunities.

Learn More

Our Kindergarten program provides a balanced curriculum focusing on foundational literacy, numeracy, and social skills. Children participate in interactive lessons, creative projects, and group activities designed to foster curiosity and confidence.

Primary

Focused teaching that develops literacy, numeracy, and problem-solving skills for future success.

Learn More

Our Primary program emphasizes core subjects such as Mathematics, English, Science, and Social Studies. Students benefit from interactive lessons, project-based learning, and extracurricular activities that foster critical thinking and personal growth.

Junior High School

Providing quality teaching and guidance to equip students for senior high school and lifelong success.

Learn More

Our Junior High School program offers a comprehensive curriculum including advanced Mathematics, Science, English, and Social Studies. Students are encouraged to participate in leadership opportunities, clubs, and competitions to prepare for senior high school and future success.
